Plan Energy Rate

AmberStream operates the AmberFalls Hydroelectric Dam, a 174 MW facility that stores water behind a dam and releases it through turbines to generate clean electricity on demand. The 174 MW figure is the instantaneous power output at a moment in time—not energy “per hour.”

When running at full output, AmberFalls can produce about 4,176 MWh of energy in a 24‑hour period. Over a typical year at ~50% capacity factor, that’s around 762,120 MWh—roughly enough for 190,530–254,040 homes.
